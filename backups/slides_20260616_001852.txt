What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.


AI Timeline
1956::The term "Artificial Intelligence" is created.
1997::IBM Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov.
2012::Deep learning becomes mainstream after ImageNet breakthroughs.
2025::AI assistants and autonomous agents become widely adopted.


AI vs Traditional Software
Traditional software follows fixed rules.|AI systems can learn patterns from data and improve over time.


Key Takeaway
AI is not replacing human intelligence—it is becoming a powerful tool that helps people work smarter.
